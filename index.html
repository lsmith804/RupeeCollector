Rupee Collector

Link will give you a random number at the start of the game.There are four rupees below. By clicking on a rupee, you will add a specific value to your score. You win the game by matching your total score to a random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each rupee is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each rupee. Good luck!
